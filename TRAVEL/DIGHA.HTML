DIGHA

Digha is a seaside resort town in the state of West Bengal at the edge of BAY OF BENGAL. Sand beaches and sweet roaring of sea waves attracts tourists from all over the globe. It's natural beauty is heart touching. Here the nature unfolds her heavenly beauty in front of mankind.There also some rare species of animals that enforce the tourists to explore her.
