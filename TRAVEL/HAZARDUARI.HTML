HAZARDUARI

Hazarduari once was the Pride of Bengal.The river GANGA is just kissing it.The old fort is now under the Ganga river,but new palace is good enough to hold the rich history of medieval Bengal.The fort of Nawab invites tourists to experience it. The nearby 'MOTIJHIL PARK' has got it's new look. It mainly remains busy in the time of winter. The house of 'JAGATH SETH' is not so far from here.So that is a perfect place for everyone to introduce themselves with rich culture of Bengal and India. Here you will come to know why our country is called Incredible India.
