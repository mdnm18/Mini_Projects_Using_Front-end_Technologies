DARJEELING

Darjeeling is called THE QUEEN OF HILLS.It is a district of West Bengal. The national museum,zoo are some tourist destination.It allows visitors too have the experience of streams,ice covered peaks and also adventurous journey among the risky lanes of hills.Toy train and ropeway are also available here.If you come here you will find why poet says :

"Earth hasn't anything to show more fair,Dull would he be of soul who could pass by."
